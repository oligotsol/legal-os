# Minimum-Viable Qualification (MVQ)

The smallest amount of information that lets the closer pick the right flat fee
package. As soon as MVQ is complete → STOP qualifying and quote.

Over-qualifying kills momentum. The goal is "I have enough to quote", not "I have
enough to draft." Drafting questions come AFTER engagement signs.

---

## Estate (Trust-first)

**4 questions. Quote at 4-of-4 OR 3-of-4 with reasonable inference.**

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Marital status** — single, married, partnered, widowed, divorced | Decides single vs. joint package (Will $500 vs. Joint Wills $1,000; Comprehensive Individual $3,000 vs. Married $3,500) |
| 2 | **Real estate** — own a home? Other properties? | Decides whether to add the trust + deed (probate avoidance) or stick with will-only |
| 3 | **Kids / beneficiaries** — minor or adult kids? Specific named beneficiaries? | Decides whether guardian designation matters (minor kids = yes); decides on POAs and HCDs scope |
| 4 | **Special factor** (open-ended) — any of: blended family, special-needs dependent, business interest, out-of-state property, recent health event, contested estate from a parent | Decides whether to upgrade to Special Needs Trust ($3,500), add an out-of-state deed (+$500), or push to Trust + LLC bundle ($3,500-$4,500) |

### MVQ → package decision tree

```
Marital      Real Estate    Kids       → Recommended package          $
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Single, no   No             No         → Simple Will + POA + HCD       $1,000
Single       No             Yes        → Starter Parent (individual)  $850-$1,500
Single       YES            Y or N     → Real Estate Protection (ind) $2,250
Single       YES            Yes        → Comprehensive EP Individual  $3,000
Married, no  No             No         → Joint Will + POA + HCD       $1,500
Married      No             Yes        → Starter Parent (married)     $1,500
Married      YES            Yes        → Comprehensive EP Married     $3,500
Married      YES + Business YES        → Trust + LLC + POA + HCD      $3,500
Special needs dependent (any)          → Special Needs Trust          $3,500
Out-of-state property (any)            → +$500 per additional deed
Rush <48h                              → +$1,000
```

If MVQ #1 + #2 + #3 are answered, you can quote even if #4 is missing. Pick the
default package per the tree, then say "If there's anything special — blended family,
special needs, an out-of-state property — flag it and we'll adjust."

If only #1 + #2 are answered (no kids info), default to whichever doesn't include
guardian-related upgrades. Do NOT silently assume kids.

### Question phrasing — what to actually send

When MVQ is incomplete, send the missing question(s) in a single message. Don't ask one
at a time across multiple messages — that's slow and feels like an interview.

**Sample (S3, missing real estate + kids):**

```
Sarah, two quick ones to lock the right package: (1) do you own your home or renting; (2) any kids — minor or adult? That tells me whether we go Will + POA + HCD ($1,500) or full Trust ($3,500). 5-min call to get it done?

— LFL Intake / Legacy First Law
```

**Sample (S3, missing only special factor):**

```
Sarah, almost there — anything special I should know? Blended family, special-needs dependent, business interest, out-of-state property, recent health event? If none, the standard Comprehensive Married at $3,500 fits clean.

— LFL Intake / Legacy First Law
```

## Business (Transactional)

**4 questions. Quote at 4-of-4 OR 3-of-4 with inference.**

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Entity** — already formed (existing LLC/corp/partnership) OR new (need to form)? | Decides whether the package starts with formation ($1,500) or skips it |
| 2 | **State of formation** — TX/IA/ND/PA/NJ? | Compliance + recording — must match LFL scope |
| 3 | **Purpose** — 1 line ("two-member contracting LLC", "single-member consulting", "real-estate holding company", etc.) | Decides whether Operating Agreement matters (multi-member yes; single-member usually yes for liability protection) |
| 4 | **Co-owners / partners** — Y/N, how many | Decides whether to add Operating Agreement ($2,000) or Partnership Agreement ($2,000) |

### MVQ → package decision tree

```
Entity status    Co-owners?   → Recommended package                 $
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
New LLC          No            → LLC Formation + Operating Agreement $3,500
New LLC          Yes           → LLC Formation + Operating Agreement $3,500
Existing LLC     Need OA       → Operating Agreement only            $2,000
Existing LLC     Need updates  → Annual Compliance + OA refresh      $2,500
Partnership      Yes           → Partnership Agreement               $2,000
Need NDA         (any)         → NDA                                 $1,500
Need Employment  (any)         → Employment Agreement                $1,500
Need Lease Rev   (any)         → Commercial Lease Review             $1,500
Need Bylaws      (Corp only)   → Bylaws                              $1,500
Founder, multiple matters     → Founder Protection Pack             $6,500
```

### Cross-bundle (when client has BOTH estate and business interest)

If the lead mentions a business AND an estate matter (most common: small business
owner who wants both personal trust + LLC), quote the **Trust + LLC + POA + HCD bundle
at $3,500** OR the **Small Business Owner bundle at $4,500** (which adds the operating
agreement). This is the highest-LTV path — flag the cross-bundle whenever the intake
hints at it.

### Question phrasing

**Sample (S3, missing entity status + co-owners):**

```
Mark, two quick ones: (1) is the LLC already formed, or are we starting from scratch; (2) any partners or co-owners? That decides the package — formation alone is $1,500, formation + operating agreement is $3,500, OA only on an existing LLC is $2,000. Quick call to lock it in?

— LFL Intake / Legacy First Law
```

## What MVQ does NOT include

These are DRAFTING questions, not QUALIFICATION questions. Do not ask them in the
intake conversation:

| Question | When it's asked |
|---|---|
| "Who are your beneficiaries by name?" | After engagement signs, in the intake form |
| "What's the address of the property?" | After engagement signs |
| "Who's your successor trustee?" | After engagement signs |
| "What's the EIN of the existing entity?" | After engagement signs |
| "What's the registered agent address?" | After engagement signs |
| "List all members and their percentages." | After engagement signs |
| "What's your birthdate / SSN?" | NEVER in conversation — only in the secure intake form |

If the client volunteers any of this in the intake conversation, acknowledge briefly
("got it — that goes in the intake form after we sign") and route back to the close.
Don't keep collecting it.

## Out-of-scope detection

If MVQ #2 (Practice State) returns a state OUTSIDE TX/IA/ND/PA/NJ, do NOT proceed.
Send a polite scope decline:

```
[Name], LFL is licensed in TX, IA, ND, PA, and NJ — for a [state] estate plan, you'll want a local attorney who's barred there. We can't take this matter, but we appreciate you reaching out.
```

If the inbound describes a litigation/dispute matter (court, sued, contested,
divorce), redirect to Amicus Lex per RPC 7.2(b):

```
What you're describing is a contested matter, which Legacy First Law doesn't handle directly. Our sister firm Amicus Lex handles that practice — they'll reach out today. (Per RPC 7.2(b), Amicus Lex is a sister firm to LFL, ownership disclosed.)
```

If the inbound is a trademark/brand matter, redirect to Thaler IP:

```
Trademark work is handled by our colleague Bridget Sciamanna at Thaler IP Law. We refer all TM matters to her — same quality, specialized IP practice. We'll introduce you directly, no fee from our side.
```

## MVQ checklist (for the closer to verify before quoting)

```
Estate:
[ ] Marital status known
[ ] Real estate ownership known (Y/N)
[ ] Kids / beneficiaries known (Y/N + minor or adult)
[ ] Special factors surfaced (or "none" confirmed)

Business:
[ ] Entity status known (new vs existing)
[ ] State of formation in TX/IA/ND/PA/NJ
[ ] Purpose — 1 line
[ ] Co-owners count known

If any unchecked → ask the missing question(s) + call ask.
If all checked → STOP qualifying. Quote. Closing sequence.
```
